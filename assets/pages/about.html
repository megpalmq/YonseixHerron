About the Collaboration

Yonsei University and Herron School of Art + Design students unite to create impactful designs addressing food scarcity and health challenges for elderly communities in Korea and Indianapolis. Each team was assigned a distinct topic and presentation style, using visuals, typography, and layout to communicate the message effectively.

This collaboration was a valuable opportunity for students to learn from one another, explore diverse design techniques, and gain hands-on experience in addressing critical social issues through creative storytelling.

Food Insecurity in Indiana & Korea

Food insecurity affects vulnerable populations across Indiana and Korea, particularly the elderly and underserved communities. By focusing on these regions, our collaboration highlights the importance of addressing food access disparities and fostering community-driven solutions.
